Santhosh Prabhu

Santhosh Prabhu

I am a PhD student at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, advised by Prof. Matthew Caesar. My research interest is in using Formal Methods to create dependable networks. I also work closely with Veriflow, a startup that is making network verification accessible to enterprises. Before UIUC, I was a Master's student at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, advised by Prof. Pallab Dasgupta.
