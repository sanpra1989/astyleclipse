ABOUt ME


I am a PhD candidate at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign, advised by Prof. Matthew Caesar. My research focuses on the use of Formal Methods to create dependable networks. I also work closely with Veriflow, a startup making network verificaiton accessible to enterprises.  Before UIUC, I obtained my Master's degree from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, advised by Prof. Pallab Dasgupta.
